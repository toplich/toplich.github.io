tmux (terminal multiplexer) lets you run multiple terminal sessions in one window ‚Äî detach and reattach them at will. It’s ideal for remote work and multitasking in the terminal.

1

This creates a new tmux session named mysession.

üîÅ Reattach to a Session

Attach to a specific session:

1
